## Full Description (4000 characters max)

```
THE CONVERGENCE IS COMING. YOU ARE THE LAST SIGNAL.

Defend the Nexus Grid — humanity's final relay station — against endless waves of corrupted machine drones. Place towers, discover synergies, and outsmart an enemy that learns from your every move.

ENEMIES THAT ADAPT
Last Signal's signature mechanic: The Convergence evolves. Rely too much on one damage type and enemies gain resistance to it. Switch strategies, diversify your towers, or watch your defenses crumble. Every run is a shifting puzzle.

7 TOWERS, ENDLESS COMBINATIONS
• Pulse Cannon — Raw single-target firepower
• Arc Emitter — Chain lightning across groups
• Cryo Array — Freeze and slow enemies to a crawl
• Missile Pod — Devastating area-of-effect splash damage
• Beam Spire — Piercing beam that cuts through enemies
• Nano Hive — Support aura that boosts nearby towers
• Harvester — Generates bonus gold from kills

Each tower has a branching 3-tier upgrade tree with 8 possible evolutions. Your build is never the same twice.

TOWER SYNERGIES
Place specific towers near each other to unlock powerful combo effects. Cryo + Arc = Shatter. Pulse + Missile = Barrage. Discover all 8 hidden synergies.

6 ACTIVE ABILITIES
Orbital Strike, EMP Burst, Shield Matrix, and more. Activate devastating powers in clutch moments to turn the tide.

46-LEVEL CAMPAIGN
Battle through 5 regions with escalating difficulty. Fixed paths, maze maps, multi-entry levels — every region brings new challenges and unlocks new towers.

ENDLESS MODE
Procedurally generated waves that never stop. Elite enemies with special modifiers appear from wave 15. Every 5 waves, choose from roguelite buff cards that stack for your entire run. How far can you push?

DAILY CHALLENGES
A new challenge every day with a fixed seed — everyone plays the same map and constraints. Restricted towers, economy runs, survival mode, speed runs, boss rush. Build streaks for bonus rewards.

DEEP META-PROGRESSION
• Skill trees for each tower type (10 nodes each)
• 8 global upgrades that carry across all runs
• Tower mastery system — track lifetime stats, unlock permanent bonuses
• Abilities to unlock and upgrade

SIGNAL DECODE MINIGAME
Between waves, intercept Convergence transmissions. Decode signal sequences for bonus gold, damage buffs, and cooldown reductions.

PLAY YOUR WAY
• 3 difficulty modes: Normal, Hard, Nightmare
• Offline — no internet required
• No energy system — play as much as you want
• Optional rewarded ads for bonus diamonds

Built with love using procedural geometric visuals and adaptive synthesized audio. No sprites, no samples — everything generated in real-time.

Defend the last signal. Adapt or fall.
```
